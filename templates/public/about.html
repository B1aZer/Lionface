About Lionface
There are three primary goals of Lionface: maintain an environment free of visual clutter (specifically in regards to advertisements), protect and respect people's privacy down to the smallest detail, and contribute to the community in a meaningful and positive way.

Lionface is built in Python Django.

Albums

Albums on Lionface are more comprehensive than traditional. Instead of just compiling images into Albums, say from a trip to Germany, you can attach multiple photos (and videos) to a post, write what you want, and then compile those posts into Albums. You could have a post for your first day in Germany, with photos and videos, and then a post from your visit to Hiedelberg Castle in another post, attach these posts to your Germany Album and the presentation and organization becomes much cleaner and more efficient. Or, have an Album just for your poetry, or favorite websites.

Lionface does not currently support videos directly. Videos can be embedded from sites like Youtube and Vimeo.

Degree of Separation

Degree of Separation represents the number of people that separate you from any given person on Lionface. For example, the Degree of Separation between you and your friend is 0 because you know them directly. The Degree of Separation between you and your friend's friend is 1, because your friend stands between you and them. Your friend's friend's friend and you have a Degree of Separation of 2, and so on.

Pages

Pages are an important feature of Lionface, and there's a lot you can do with them.
Loving By loving a Page, you'll receive a Page's Updates in your news feed, and they will be listed under the Loves tab on your profile. You'll also have access to their Discussions, Calendar and Community pages.

If you're trying to Love a Business Page, and the Business is listed as inactive, then go ahead and Love the Page anyway. You will be added to their waiting list, and when they become active again your Love will be added to their tally, their updates will appear in your news feed, they will be listed under the Loves tab on your profile. You'll also have access to their Discussions, Calendar and Community Pages.
Updates Pages can post Updates, which you'll receive in your news feed.
Discussions Here you can discuss topics related to the Page.
If you are an Admin, there are special privacy settings that allow discussions to take place between your Page and other Pages (that you're friends with) or with the other Admins, Employees, Interns or Volunteers of your Page.
Feedback Businesses and Nonprofits alike need our feedback to grow and improve their organization.
Calendar This is a great feature that allows us all to keep up with events in real time.
Community You can identify yourself with a Page as either an Employee or Intern, or if it's a Nonprofit, as a volunteer. If you're a Page Admin, you can add other Pages as friends. Users who have allowed their Loves to be publicly visible on Pages are also shown here.
